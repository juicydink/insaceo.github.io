About Us

FAMILY & FRIENDS BROUGHT TOGETHER BY FOOD IS WHY WE’RE HERE… Prime foods product, established in 2010, is one of the pioneering and most reputable restaurants in Addis Ababa, introducing proudly the Ethiopian hospitality through diverse cuisines and well serving. In a cozy environment, designed and built in a fancy way, prime foods serves the varieties of local and international dishes and drinks along with spectacular music. Prime foods products, symbolizes hard work, dedication, fearlessness, and an iron will. It drives us to work hard to pay homage to those who paved our way. Maybe prime foods was not seen as our crew’s crowning achievement, but to us, it is the unrecognized leap of faith that has shaped who we are today and continues to inspire the paths to who we will become
